About Rank Kimchi

Rank Kimchi was created for the Korean movie enthusiast. Focused on the modern cinematography of Korea, users can be critics on our featured selected titles. Users will be able to critique not only movies, but also other users. This way common users can gain authority and a voice by ranking higher. Our ranks will include critic ranking, movie ranking, and movie critique rankings. Feel free to leave your input, because everyone else will!

How to Rank Kimchi

In Rank Kimchi, we wanted to provide our users with an easy way to create polls on almost everything. Our highest ranks and will appear in our rankiest ranks based on the categories: movie, critic, or critique. It's very easy! Just press the to rank someone up, or press to rank down. We set up crazy algorithms to automatically create polls, so you don't have to do the math. All you have to do is write, vote, or both!
